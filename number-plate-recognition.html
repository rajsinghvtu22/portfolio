Problem

Vehicle identification from video is noisy and error-prone.

Number plates appear at different angles, distances, lighting conditions, and motion blur levels. Extracting reliable text requires more than a single OCR call.

Solution

A staged OCR pipeline for plate detection and validation.

The system locates plate regions, normalizes the image crop, extracts text, validates plate formats, and prepares the result for vehicle identification or downstream review.

Challenges

Motion blur

Vehicle speed can degrade plate readability, making preprocessing and confidence scoring essential.

Plate variation

Fonts, dirt, glare, and physical damage require robust OCR normalization steps.

False positives

Text-like regions on vehicles must be filtered with plate-shape and format validation.

Lessons Learned

  • Preprocessing drives OCR quality. Denoising, contrast, and ROI cleanup can matter as much as the OCR engine itself.
  • Validation protects downstream systems. Format checks and confidence thresholds reduce bad IDs before lookup.
  • Review workflows matter. Low-confidence reads need a clean human-in-the-loop path.
