Problem

Manual traffic monitoring is slow and inconsistent.

Traffic teams need a way to identify vehicle movement patterns and possible violations from camera feeds without depending only on manual observation.

Solution

Automated detection plus rule-based event logic.

The system detects vehicles in each frame, tracks their location relative to monitoring zones, and creates violation events for review when rule conditions are met.

Challenges

Camera perspective

Violation regions depend heavily on camera placement, requiring configurable monitoring zones.

Occlusion

Dense traffic can hide vehicles, so confidence thresholds and review queues are important.

Rule precision

Violation logic must be transparent so human reviewers understand why an event was flagged.
